Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our services.

1. How can I book tickets online?

You can book tickets by visiting our website and selecting your desired movie, date, time, and seats. Follow the simple steps to complete the payment securely.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile wallet payments, and online banking options for your convenience.

3. Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 24 hours before the showtime. Refunds or changes will be processed in accordance with our refund policy.

4. Are there discounts for students or senior citizens?

Yes, we offer discounts for students and senior citizens. Please present valid identification at the time of booking or check-in.

5. Are food and drinks allowed inside the cinema?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the cinema. However, you can enjoy a wide variety of snacks and beverages available at our concession stands.

6. What safety measures are in place at the cinema?

Your safety is our priority. We follow strict hygiene protocols, including regular sanitization, contactless check-ins, and adherence to local health guidelines.

7. How can I provide feedback or file a complaint?

You can provide feedback or file a complaint by visiting our Feedback page or contacting us directly via email at support@abccinema.com.
